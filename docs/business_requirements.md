# Business Requirements Document

## 1. Business Problem

Olist is an e-commerce marketplace that connects sellers with customers across Brazil. The business needs a clear analytics solution to understand sales performance, customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, payment behavior, and seller contribution.

Currently, the raw data is spread across multiple CSV files, making it difficult to quickly answer business questions and monitor performance.

## 2. Project Objective

The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end Power BI dashboard that helps business users analyze:

- Revenue performance
- Order trends
- Product and category performance
- Customer distribution
- Seller performance
- Delivery performance
- Payment behavior
- Review and satisfaction patterns

## 3. Target Users

This dashboard is designed for:

- Business managers
- Sales managers
- Operations managers
- Delivery/logistics teams
- Customer experience teams
- Data analysts

## 4. Key Business Questions

The dashboard should answer the following business questions:

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. How many orders were placed?
3. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
4. Which states or cities have the highest customer demand?
5. Which sellers contribute the most to revenue?
6. What is the average delivery time?
7. How often are orders delivered late?
8. Which payment methods are most commonly used?
9. What is the average customer review score?
10. Is there a relationship between delivery delay and review score?

## 5. Initial KPI List

The initial KPIs planned for this project are:

- Total Revenue
- Total Sales
- Total Freight Value
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Sellers
- Total Products
- Average Order Value
- Average Review Score
- Average Delivery Days
- Late Delivery Rate
- Cancelled Orders
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue by State
- Orders by Payment Type
- Review Score Distribution

## 6. Planned Dashboard Pages

The Power BI report will include the following pages:

1. Executive Overview
2. Sales Performance
3. Product and Category Analysis
4. Customer and Location Analysis
5. Seller Performance
6. Delivery and Freight Analysis
7. Payment Analysis
8. Review and Satisfaction Analysis
9. Drill-through Order Details

## 7. Expected Outcome

The final dashboard should help users understand business performance, identify high-performing categories and sellers, monitor delivery issues, analyze customer satisfaction, and support data-driven decision-making.
